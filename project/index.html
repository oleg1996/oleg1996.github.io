ABOUT WABAGRID

We are a new marketplace for those who seek to explore less mainstream but just as good, if not better quality, everyday products.

We want to change this by giving the small businesses a chance. Besides big name corporations who mass produce products, there are so many amazing small companies that are not as well known to the public and we want to let the people discover and support these companies.

As consumers, extra profits can be made super easy just by promoting products of your liking to your friends and guests.

The best part? With Wabagrid, this is possible from your computer. No need to travel far and wide to get awesome products from around the world.
